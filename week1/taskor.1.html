CRISTIANO RONALDO 
    1985 - Present

The Best Player In The World

Cristiano Ronaldo, the renowned Portuguese professional footballer, has had an illustrious career. He began at Sporting CP in Portugal and rose to prominence at Manchester United, winning three English Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League. His most prolific years were at Real Madrid, where he became the club's all-time leading scorer and secured four Champions League titles. Later, he joined Juventus in Serie A, and in 2021, Ronaldo returned to Manchester United. Currently, he plays for Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr. On the international stage, Ronaldo has been a key player for the Portugal national team, achieving success in UEFA European Championships and the UEFA Nations League. With five Ballon d'Or titles, he's one of the greatest footballers of all time, having scored over 700 career goals. His impact on the game is truly legendary!
